How To Buy?

Step 1

Buy BNB coins through Binance exchange.

Step 2

Download the Metamask extension to your smartphone or Mozilla FireFox browser. Setup a wallet on the BSC-network. Transfer BNB coins from your Binance wallet to the Metamask wallet.

Step 3

Go to Pancakeswap website and click on trade and exchange. Search for custom coin with the Smooth Coin contract address. Set slippage to 10%.
